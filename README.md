## About

CodeCanon is a software consulting and development company that delivers high-quality software solutions across three tiers:

- **Developer Tools** — Libraries, components, and tooling for the modern developer ecosystem.
- **Business Solutions** — Tailored software for small to medium-sized businesses looking to streamline operations and scale.
- **Consumer Products** — Direct-to-consumer applications designed for everyday use.

We maintain a mix of **open source** and **premium** offerings, committed to pushing the boundaries of what great software looks like.
